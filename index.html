Welcome to a Sunsets Site ♡

As with twilight, there is civil dusk, nautical dusk, and astronomical dusk, occurring at the exact moment when the center of the sun's disk is at 6°, 12°, and 18° below the horizon!

Subscribe To Learn More About Sunsets:

Civil Dawn

Venus and Jupiter are visible at civil dawn.

Nautical Dusk

It is purple colored, and if there is some cloud or haze, there can be bronze, purple and yellow colours.

Astronomical Dusk

After astronomical dusk and before astronomical dawn, the sky is not illuminated by the Sun.
