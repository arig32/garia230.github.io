All About Sunsets!

About Us

Sunsets don't only come in just three variations they can change! In the summer, dramatic thunderstorms can be lit up in all sorts of sunset colours and create an amazing scene. In the winter, snow squalls can be lit up by the setting sun. In the summer, there tends to be a more muted tone to sunrise and sunset due to the existence of more moisture and pollution. In the winter, we tend to see much more vibrant sunrises and sunsets due to drier and clearer air.

Psychology reasearch has shown that exposure to sunsets yields leads to stress relief! So if you want to de-stress watch a sunset.

What We Do

We are a team of Sunset Experts! We hope to help people relieve stress they gather in everyday life by watching sunsets. We have a wacky job! :)
